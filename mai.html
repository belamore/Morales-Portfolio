About

MAI is an interactive visual novel I created for my senior project. The narrative revolves around the main character MAI (aka the player) who was created by a very prestigious scientist named Dr.Solis who worked with the government; When Dr. Solis discovers the government’s unethical intentions for her research, she becomes a target and the government abducts her daughter, Nira, as leverage. Determined to protect her child and prevent her work from being weaponized, Dr. Solis secretly creates MAI with a single purpose: to find and protect Nira, and discover the truth about Dr.Solis' wheareabouts.

  As the sole contributor to my senior project, I was responsible for all aspects of creating a cohesive interactive visual novel, including illustration, programming, and writing. The slideshow above features selected illustrations from the project, and the complete novel can be accessed through the link below.
