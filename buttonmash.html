About

Button Mash is a product of my interaction design class which required us to develop a game surrounding the use of the Makey Makey kit. This was a group project that consisted of 3 people for each team. For this project my team and I decided to make a game similar to "Simon Says" inspired by a "Dance Dance Revolution" aesthetic.
  My main focuses involved designing and implementing player 1 and player 2 mascots, incorperating a clear winning and losing state for players by making happy and sad versions of the mascots, programming the buttons, timer, player score, and creating the website.

How to play:

Player 1 keys: WASD
Player 2 keys: Arrows

  Two players must compete against each other to gain the most amount of points before the time runs out. Players gain points by pressing the button that correctly corresponds with the highlighted arrow before their opponent can.
If a player presses the wrong button, they are punished by a 3 second timeout that prohibits the player from pressing a button again until the end of their timeout.
Who ever gains the most points before the timer runs out wins!
