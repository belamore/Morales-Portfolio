About

Letter to Future-self was a (solo) User Experience Design project where we were required to develop a unique user experience that is in-person and does not revolve around the use of technology. Throughout this project we were required to document our findings and present these findings in the form of a slide presentation (refer to slides above).
    The experience I created is called "Letter to Future-self", where participants emotionally engage in a simple and fun self-reflection activity that allows you to communicate with your future-self in a fun way. The goal of this experience was to inspire participants to engage in self-reflection more often and better recognize the importance of it.
   As part of this project, I designed an instruction page and a custom envelope unique to this experience (seen on slides
16-18). The envelope-making process was incorporated in my final iteration to create a short crafting segment that would engage users further in the experience and promote relaxation.
