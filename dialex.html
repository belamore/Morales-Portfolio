Dialex: A Smartwatch App Design

Dialex high-fidelity wireframe.

About

  Dialex was a smartwatch app concept that was developed for a group project, where we were tasked with tackling a particular issue with the limitations of a small screen. As a result, my team and I designed an app that provides seamless, convenient translations, allowing users to communicate effortlessly with people who speak different languages—right from their wrist.
   While Dialex was targeted to those who love to socialize and frequently travel to foreign countries, it is useful for anyone looking to connect with speakers of other languages through a more accessible approach—whether to build relationships or even to learn a new language along the way.
  Our process involved identifying a problem, researching relevant statistics for the purpose of our app such as the percentage of people who speak more or less than one language and the frequency of translator usage among users, creating a user persona for our app, and more.
  Regarding my primary roles in this project, I was responsible for managing the project schedule leading up to the final submission and designing the high-fidelity wireframe.
