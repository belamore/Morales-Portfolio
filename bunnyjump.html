About

Bunny Jump is a platformer game that is a product of my interaction design class which required us to develop a simple game. This assignment was a two-person group project. My partner and I decided that we would do a platformer game with a cute aesthetic so we decided on a warm color palette along with a pixelated look.
    My main focuses were designing and programming the assets of the game. These assets include: the player character (bunny), the enemy character (wolf), the bouncy mushroom, grass platforms, and the carrots (point system).

How to play:

Player keys: Arrow keys or WASD

 In Bunny Jump, you gets to play as cute pink bunny exploring the wilderness. The players mission is to reach the end of the level by avoiding enemies (wolves) and using mushrooms to get to high platforms while collecting as many carrots you can!
