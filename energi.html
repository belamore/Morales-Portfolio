Energi: An AR App Concept

About

Energi is a sustainable energy mobile AR app concept, designed around SDG Goal #7 which is related to affordable, reliable, and sustainable energy development.
   This was a final project for my UX Design class which required us to create a concept for an AR App based on one of the 17 sustainable energy goals adopted by the United Nations. My group chose to design our concept around SDG Goal #7 - a goal focused on ensuring further accessibility to sustianable energy; more information regarding this goal can be found here.
   In regards to our process, this project required us to conduct research on the topic of sustainable energy, conduct interviews with college students to inquire on their knowledge of the topic as our target audience, and using this information to determine the foundation of our app and begin prototyping, leading to our final high-fidelity prototype above designed through figma.
