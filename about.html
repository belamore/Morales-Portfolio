About Me!

    Hello, my name is Belen Morales, and welcome to my portfolio! I am a fourth-year student attending Cal State University, East Bay, pursuing a BFA in Interaction & Game Design, and projected to graduate in Fall 2025.
    My experience at Cal State East Bay has helped me to develop and continuously refine my skills in Game Design, Interaction Design, User Experience Design, Web Design, and multimedia editing using Adobe Creative Suite. Additionally, my time at CSUEB has allowed me to strengthen my abilities in teamwork, time management, and problem-solving.
    Outside of my education, I (of course) have a deep passion for art and video games—these are both hobbies I developed at a very young age. My preferred art medium is digital, and I primarily use procreate for my artworks. When it comes to gaming, my all-time favorite titles are Final Fantasy VII and Baldur's Gate 3, and I also enjoy FPS games such as Valorant.
  Needless to say, I'm proud to have pursued my dream career and feel enthusiastic about the journey ahead!
